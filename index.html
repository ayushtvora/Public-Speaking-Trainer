What is [Product Name]?

[Product Name] is a two-minute exercise that will help you get better at public speaking and speaking on the spot. It will help you build confidence and reinforce speaking habits by identifying stuttering and filler words, and repetition. [Product Name] will also check for good posture to make sure you are looking and speaking as confidently as you can.

First, you will have one minute to prepare. You will be given a topic, and you can start brainstorming ideas on what to talk about to prepare for the exercise. After the minute, you will have a chance to speak about your topic. Try to speak until the end of the limit, but don't go overboard!

How do you use it?

  1. Make sure that your camera and microphone are working.
  2. Get prepared! You may want to have a pen and some paper handy to take notes. Also drink some water and mentally prepare yourself.
  3. Once you are ready, press the "Get Started!" button above. The timer will start immediately, and you will be given your topic
  4. Start brainstorming ideas for what you want to talk about in your speech. Remember, you don't have too much time!
  5. A few seconds before the timer ends, prepare yourself to start speaking. Once the timer ends, the recording will immediately begin
  6. Start Speaking! Stay confident and speak clearly. The recording will automatically stop after one minute.
  7. Check out the analytics. It will give you a score to track your progress and give you advice on how you can improve.
  8. OPTIONAL: You can download your recording to watch yourself. By doing this, you can identify some other mistakes that you may want to improve on.
